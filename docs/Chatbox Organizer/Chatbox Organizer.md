## Overview

Chatbox Organizer is an application designed to capture, select, and organize parts of explanatory texts.  
The goal is to provide a simple tool for students and professionals who want to save only the most useful excerpts, keeping their notes organized in personalized categories.

---

## Objective

Allow the user to select parts of texts, save these selections into thematic boxes, and easily view, edit, or delete them.  
The main idea is to make the study and review process more efficient and focused.
